In January 2015, bulldozers and caterpillars arrived along the littoral between Abidjan and Grand-Bassam and started demolishing houses.

There were over 4,000 houses in this 4.2-mile highway.

22,000 people lost their home.

Ever since then, there has been an increase in the speed and frequency of residence destruction.
